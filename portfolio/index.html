Portfolio

I have curated several latest projects that I have led or participated in recent years, most of them comes from 'SVW mobile app & HMI system'. You can check the released product via IOS/Android/Production car. The case study however, mainly focus on demonstraing the design rationale, process and philosophy.

Charging Experience

As electric car turns to be a new form of transportation, our users are facing a new experience - Charging. In this project, my team designed the holistic charging experience for our MEB vehicles.

Digital Key

Mobile has redesigned how people get access to their wallets in China, it will also replace the key fob with the development of bluetooth & UWB tech. So we are trying to build an intuitive experience for them to get into the car.

Remote AC Control

In our contextual interview with few users, we surprisingly find out that they have such a strong need to remotely open up their AC in advance, which could help them survive from certain circumstances, and there we go!

Rental Car App

This is an internal application that aims to help the employees find available rental cars in the designated area. They could use the car for business travel or personal use, and the app tries to improve the efficiency of the reimbursement procedure as well.

Fare Analysis

The feature is quite clear: to help users record and display all the fares that relevant to their vehicles. The real questions for the design: a. Was the data simple enough for users to understand? b. Was it accurate and smart enough for users to trust?

Fuel Economy Analysis

This feature is also related to 'Fare Analysis', which our target is to help our users have a better understanding about their fuel economy comsumption and internally create a precise tag of each user by our operation team.
